URLS:
-->Frontend
    Home: http://localhost:3000/
    About: http://localhost:3000/about
    Contact: http://localhost:3000/contact
    jobSearch: http://localhost:3000/jobSearch
    Profile: http://localhost:3000/profile
    Post: http://localhost:3000/post: Can post a job but at the moment it is hardcoded to save it for username='tuna2'
    Register: http://localhost:3000/register: Creates a new user
    Job Detail View: http://localhost:3000/jobDetail/job_id You can also delete that job from this view
    Job Delete: http://localhost:3000/jobDelete/job_id
    404: http://localhost:3000/*else

-->Backend
    404: http://127.0.0.1:8000/
    login: http://127.0.0.1:8000/login
    logout: http://127.0.0.1:8000/logout
    Returns All Posted Jobs: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/jobSearch
    Creates a JobPost: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/post-new-job
    Returns Details of a JobPost: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/jobDetail/<int:job_id>
    Delets a JobPost: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/jobDelete/<int:job_id>
